Global Methane Emissions

Methane emissions from the energy sector declined by 10% in 2024, marking significant progress toward the Global Methane Pledge targets. Improved leak detection and repair programs contributed to these reductions.

Key Developments

  • Satellite monitoring coverage expanded to 80% of oil/gas fields
  • New regulations required 95% methane capture at landfills
  • Coal mine methane capture increased by 25%
  • Agricultural methane remained challenging to reduce
