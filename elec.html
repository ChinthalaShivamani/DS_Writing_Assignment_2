Global electricity generation, consumption and renewable transition in 2024

Global Electricity Trends

Electricity demand grew by 4.3% in 2024, nearly twice as fast as overall energy demand. Renewables accounted for a record 42% of global electricity generation, surpassing coal for the first time in history.

Key Developments

  • Solar PV capacity grew by 45% year-on-year
  • Data center electricity demand reached 3.5% of global total
  • Grid-scale battery storage capacity tripled
  • Electrification of transport and heating accelerated
